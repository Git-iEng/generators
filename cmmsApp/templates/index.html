Our Process

Generator Project Workflow

A clear, standards-driven workflow for sizing, supplying, installing, and commissioning diesel, gas, and hybrid generators, delivering efficiency, safety, and long service life.

View Projects

  1. Requirements & Standards Review

    Power ratings, voltage/frequency, operating hours, environmental considerations, safety standards, and compliance checks.

  2. Site Assessment & Integration

    Load calculation, fuel requirements, ventilation, exhaust, noise, and space planning.

  3. Factory Tests, Delivery & Installation

    FAT witnessing, site delivery, proper foundation/mounting, cabling, fuel connections, earthing, and safety barriers.

  4. Commissioning & Handover

    System testing, trial run, load performance verification, SCADA/control integration, alarms & interlocks check, and O&M documentation with training.
